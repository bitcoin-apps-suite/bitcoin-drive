What is Bitcoin Drive?

Bitcoin Drive is a decentralized content monetization platform built on the BitcoinSV blockchain. Store your music, videos, images, and documents permanently on-chain while selling access to your content and issuing revenue shares to investors.

Content creators can sell access to their work through paywalls and issue tokenized shares in individual assets. Revenue-generating content automatically distributes dividends to shareholders, creating a new model for content investment and creator funding.

Files are stored immutably on the BSV blockchain with cryptographic security. Creators retain full ownership while benefiting from transparent, automated revenue distribution and permanent content preservation.
